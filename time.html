Managing Time during Lockdown

All schools, offices, parks and malls being shut due to lockdown, has left us overwhelmed with a lot of time at hand. This is definitely the time to relax and slow life down. However, we might end up spending our whole day lying on the bed scrolling feeds on our phone or playing games.


We might feel like sleeping in today and then the next day and then the next day, too. We might end up spending a lot of time watching TV, getting updates on the pandemic or enjoying the new web series. We might also feel anxious and frustrated due to limited interaction with our friends, colleagues or even family. This might leave us unmotivated and our quarantine days unproductive. 

Check out few ways in which you can maximize your time.

1. Note down all the new activities you want to do. Include those, you have been longing to do but have not been able to find time to do. 
For example, reading a book, learning a new recipe, learning a choreography, exercising, doing yoga, calling up a friend you have not been able to talk to for long, painting. 

2. Make a routine. Add one of these activities to your routine consisting of everyday tasks such as cooking, cleaning and washing etc. 
Help children to make their own to-do list and encourage them to follow it.

3. Set a time limit for each task. Keep the routine simple with doable number of tasks. Do little, everyday.

4. Take breaks. It is easy to avoid breaks when one is working from home. Breaks like making lunch, spending time with the family members, sitting in your garden can make you feel recharged. 

5. Wake up in the morning and sleep at night at the same usual time. Being consistent in daily activities makes bodies function better.
