Matters of the Mind

 

Shifting your Thoughts

Sitting at home can lead to changes in our thoughts constantly. Be it everyday, or every hour. Some of these thoughts may be helpful, while others may be unhelpful thoughts. These unhelpful thoughts may become constant or repetitive. Therefore it becomes essential to monitor our thoughts and regulate them.

Thought monitoring chart is one of the ways to keep a track of your negative thoughts and seeing what can be the positive alternative. 

A positive alternative thought must be specific, must not include "no" or "not" and must be in the present continuous tense. Moreover, it would be better to avoid words like "should" and "must" as it puts pressure on oneself. 

A positive alternative thought is not one which is diagonally opposite to the negative thought you are experiencing.

For example, if one had a negative thought that everything is bad, the alternative positive thought is NOT "Everything is good" because that would be a lie.

Rather, it is a way of reframing your thoughts, by saying that, "There are certain aspects (specify what) which are positive in my life."

Help yourself. Help others around you. Your mental health matters.
