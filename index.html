Mental Health Matters

A Space to Share, Unlearn and Learn

Care in Crisis was born at the time the world first faced the global pandemic COVID-19. It began as an initiative to help individuals cope with every day stressors associated with lockdown and to engage in self-help activities. It moved beyond having a one-way interaction to conducting group sessions and personal sessions.
We, at Care in Crisis, believe that you can make your everyday better through the process of understanding yourself, unlearning patterns and learning new ways to live a more fulfilling life; and you don't need a crisis to make that change.
