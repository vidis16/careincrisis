ABOUT US

We, through Care in Crisis, aim to provide support by lending you a listening ear to share your emotions, and also show you ways to keep yourself engaged as you spend your days at home.  
We are a group of people who care about each one who is experiencing mental and emotional turmoil and are concerned about their well-being.
We are formally trained in providing psychological support and counselling.
