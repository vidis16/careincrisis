Self Compassion: A Life Skill is a FREE workshop to build a compassionate world together.

Self compassion is the ability to be understanding, caring and warm to ourselves when one feels like a failure, feels inadequate etc. We believe this is a vital skill that we can all develop.
The workshop 'Self Compassion: A Life skill' is the first step towards building a more compassionate world. Compassion that starts from oneself.

It will be facilitated on 11th Oct (Sunday) & 17th Oct (Saturday) 2020.
